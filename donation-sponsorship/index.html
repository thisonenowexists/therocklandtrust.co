Rockland Trust Bank is committed to supporting and investing in the communities we serve. If your organization would like to be considered for a donation or sponsorship, please complete and submit our request for community support. Organizations should be located within the bank’s primary markets and assessment areas. Requests are preferred from non-profit organizations with a 501(c)3 status and will be reviewed in accordance with the bank’s annual budget. All requests will be considered based on a variety of factors including budget availability, community impact and employee or customer involvement. It may take several weeks to review a request for community support, so we recommend submitting your request 60 to 90 days before your event or project deadline. This will provide adequate time to review and respond to your request in the event it is approved. Submission of a request for community support does not guarantee that it will be approved or funded at the level requested.
